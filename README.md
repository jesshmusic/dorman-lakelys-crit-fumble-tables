# Dorman Lakely's Critical Hit & Fumble Tables

Add dramatic flair to your D&D 5e combat with tier-based critical hit and fumble tables that automatically trigger when you roll a natural 20 or natural 1!

## What Does This Module Do?

When you land a critical hit or fumble an attack, this module automatically rolls on a custom table to determine what happens. Effects range from "nothing special" to devastating blows that knock enemies prone, deal bonus damage, or even damage your own weapon!

### Tier System

The tables scale with your character's power level:

| Tier | Character Level | "Nothing Special" Chance |
| ---- | --------------- | ------------------------ |
| 1    | Levels 1-4      | 60%                      |
| 2    | Levels 5-8      | 45%                      |
| 3    | Levels 9-12     | 35%                      |
| 4    | Levels 13-20    | 25%                      |

Higher level characters face more dramatic consequences - both good and bad!

### Attack Types

Different tables for different attacks:

- **Melee** - Swords, axes, fists, and other close-combat weapons
- **Ranged** - Bows, crossbows, thrown weapons
- **Spells** - Both melee and ranged spell attacks

## Requirements

- FoundryVTT v13 or higher
- D&D 5e game system
- **Midi-QOL module** (required for automatic detection)

## Installation

1. Open Foundry VTT
2. Go to **Add-on Modules** tab
3. Click **Install Module**
4. Search for "Dorman Lakely's Critical Hit & Fumble Tables" or paste:
   ```
   https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jesshmusic/dorman-lakelys-crit-fumble-tables/main/module.json
   ```
5. Click **Install**
6. Enable the module in your game world

## Settings

Find these options in **Game Settings â†’ Module Settings**:

- **Enable Module** - Turn the whole thing on or off
- **Enable Critical Hit Tables** - Toggle crit effects
- **Enable Fumble Tables** - Toggle fumble effects
- **Apply Effects Automatically** - Auto-apply conditions and damage
- **Use Actor Level for Tier** - Scale effects to character level
- **Show Chat Messages** - Display results in chat

## Example Effects

**Critical Hits might:**

- Knock the target prone
- Deal bonus damage
- Stun the enemy
- Damage their armor (-2 AC)

**Fumbles might:**

- Make you fall prone
- Drop your weapon
- Hit yourself
- Damage your weapon (-2 to attack rolls)
